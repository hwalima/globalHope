About

Our journey, our dream

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Siyezi Community Development Projects was founded in April 2020 with their first founding committee consisting of 10 individuals elected on their first meeting with the aim develop and maintain the community.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    The organisation is currently collecting from their founding City Johannesburg South Africa where it all started though there are other people from the villages currently residing in Botswana, Zimbabwe and they have a bank account with First National Bank which they use to collect the donations.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    SCDP do not have an office as yet they still hold their meetings in public spaces i.e. Parks, for scrutiny the organisations hold records since the very first day for all the collections and jobs done there are images as well as financial statements may be available on request.

  • July 2014

    Phase Two Expansion

    The company currently responsible on the day to day as well as annual financial reports is Global Hope Investments.
